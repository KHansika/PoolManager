About Us

Welcome to Aqua Flash Swimming Club

The Aqua Flash swimming club was declared open by the Hon. D.M.Jayaratne (Prime Minister of Ceylon) on the 30th of October 2014. Rainbow Aquatics is a swimming club/squad which is situated in Colombo 7. It is a squad where a young bunch of swimmers meet on a daily basis to practice and have fun at the same time. The swimming pool is used by both students and outsiders.

Vision & Mission

Vision

"To provide a wholesome swimming environment - where the JOY OF PARTICIPATION is evident."

Mission

"Train swimmers to represent their respective schools and also the country and most importantly to build their character and mold their future."

Facilities

Swimming Pool

AQUA is blessed to have two 25m pool with six lanes in each pool and have baby pool for under 10 childern.
